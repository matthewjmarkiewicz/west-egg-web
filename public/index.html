The Aegis Platform

Every dimension of
wealth, protected.

Purpose-built for families managing complex, multi-entity portfolios across real estate, private equity, fine art, and beyond.

Portfolio Command Center

Your complete financial picture — every asset class, every entity, every valuation — consolidated into a single dashboard. Real estate, private equity, art, and liquid holdings shown alongside the insurance coverage tied to each.

Insurance Coverage Intelligence

See every policy mapped to the asset it protects. Identify gaps in coverage, upcoming renewals, and mismatched limits before they become costly exposures — across all carriers and all properties in one view.

Proactive Alert Engine

Critical risks surface themselves — expiring coverage, underinsured assets, and policy anomalies are flagged before they require a call to your broker. Your advisors stay ahead of exposure, not behind it.

Secure Document Vault

Every policy declaration, property deed, appraisal, and insurer correspondence in one encrypted repository — organized by asset and entity. When a claim arises, nothing is missing and nothing is scrambled for.

Tailored Access for Every Stakeholder

Family members, trustees, advisors, and counsel each see exactly what's relevant to their role — and nothing more. Beneficiaries review their holdings; advisors manage the full picture. Confidentiality is built into the architecture.

Executive Reporting

Premium spend by carrier, coverage-to-asset ratios, claim velocity, and settlement timelines — presented in a format ready for the family council, the investment committee, or the annual review with your wealth manager.

Claims Intelligence
