Portfolio Command Center

Consolidated net worth panel spanning every asset class. Real-time valuation, coverage gap analysis, and premium drill-down by carrier — all on a single institutional-grade dashboard.

Insurance Coverage Intelligence

Map every policy to every asset. Identify uninsured or underinsured exposures before they become catastrophic. Track renewals, limits, and exclusions across all carriers in one view.

Proactive Alert Engine

Configurable severity tiers surface critical gaps, expiring coverage, and policy anomalies. Your family never faces an unexamined risk because it slipped through the cracks.

Granular Role-Based Access

From PRINCIPAL beneficiaries to institutional FIRM_ADMINs, every role sees exactly what they need — nothing more. Multi-entity scoping with tenant-isolated data across all tiers.

Real-Time Analytics
